Education

Offer for companies, schools and organizations in sustainability and zero-waste.

Zero-waste lectures

In addition to help people reduce waste, we are also actively involved in sustainability, zero-waste, minimalism, waste and its prevention, ecology, the environment and its protection. As part of raising awareness in this topics, we are also part of various events, discussions, performances, exhibitions. At the same time, we give lectures to companies, schools and other organizations that are interested in opening these topics.

What to expect

Within these lectures, we will introduce people to the issues of a specific topic, the current state in the world and in Slovakia, and we will bring useful tips applicable to the household and the work environment. The lecture can be a separate or it can be a series of lectures that follow each other. We are coming up with the latest new EkoKvíz format!

The duration is 50 minutes + discussion as standard, but we are flexible and will adapt to your requirements. You can choose from several topics, or we will tailor the lecture to your wishes, or we can fit a specific topic to the environment of your company, school and its functioning.
